COMPRION Testing Solutions

COMPRION is a privately owned, German company that provides test solutions and services for the interfaces of the connected world. With our tools, customers worldwide test smartphones, eSIMs, IoT modules and NFC devices according to the latest industry standards and beyond...

Single Source

Our inhouse hardware and software are perfectly integrated with each other. Plus, our tools span the complete product life cycle from development to certification and quality assurance.

Quality

High precision, maximum test coverage, robustness and the high level of automation give our customers the confidence to launch products and services that work smoothly: Always and everywhere.

Expertise

With more than 20 years expertise we premiered test solutions from 2G to 5G. We helped NFC to market maturity. We fought to make eSIM a success. We shaped the introduction of first global payment terminals.

Future Proof

We are heavily involved in the development of industry-wide test specifications for the latest technologies. We implemented them immediately in our tools as base for long-term customer relationships.
